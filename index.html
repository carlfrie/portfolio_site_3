About

Hello there!

First of all, thanks for coming to my website.

I'm a former EFL teacher turned front-end web developer. Teaching and web development have more in common than you think -- both require a great degree of patience, empathy, training and of course flexibility.

As a teacher, I've had the privilege of working with students from all over the world. I've also been able to teach in some amazing countries like Vietnam, Thailand and even Saudi Arabia. Living abroad has deeply impacted my views.

In 2013, I began hacking and customizing WordPress templates for my own hobby sites. Over time, I began to gain more and more knowledge of the front-end and also development. Wanting to break away from WordPress, I started teaching myself Javascript.

My path towards development may look slow, but that's often by design. As a teacher, I know that when it comes to knowledge the slow path is the most rewarding.

Most of what you'll see here are basic projects I created to test myself or scratch an itch. I'm hoping to take on client work soon and am also looking for full-time work as a junior front-end developer or engineer.
